About Photomatics

Photomatics is a modern self-photo booth that brings a unique, stylish, and fun photo-taking experience for everyone. Inspired by
classic photo booths with a touch of contemporary aesthetics, Photomatics lets you capture moments that feel personal, nostalgic, and totally Instagram-worthy.

We offer high-quality instant prints and digital downloads, complete with various filter options—from timeless black & white to soft Korean-style tones.
Each booth is designed to be user-friendly, minimalistic, and perfect for any occasion—whether it’s a solo session, a date, or a fun moment with friends.

Now available in multiple major cities like Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, and our newest location near Universitas Indonesia in Depok, Photomatics is
here to make your memories last—one photo strip at a time.

What We Offer?


  1. Aesthetic photo filters & print styles
  2. Instant QR code for digital downloads
  3. Premium paper prints
  4. Easy-to-use booth interface
  5. Stylish and cozy booth interiors
